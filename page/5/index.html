Take part in the most insane football matches you could imagine! Super Liquid Soccer is the game for true enthusiasts, who enjoy running across the court and managing teamwork to achieve the most prestigious tournaments. Lead your team of extra wobbly sportsmen to victory and deal with all the difficulties that may occur on the court.

Gameplay  Super Liquid Soccer

The system of this game is super easy and comprehensible, so you don’t really need to be a professional in this kind of sport. All you need for  Super Liquid Soccer is a desire to have fun and a little bit of dedication. Regulate each team member and move them across the playground, scoring as many goals as possible.

Remember that every match can last for a certain period of time, so make sure you do your best to win the prize. Super Liquid Soccer game lets you choose the type of competition, consisting of:

  • Pub teams
  • Amateurs
  • Professionals
  • Internationals

All of these levels differ in difficulty, so make sure you have obtained all accessible rewards in the lowest leagues before venturing for the highest peaks of your career in Super Liquid Soccer!

Manage your Squad

Not only can you govern each of the footballers, but you can also select them in the customisation menu! Pick up only the best candidates for your party and assign them with definite roles to determine their behaviour during the game. Some of them will perform the attack and some of them will stand near the gates as a defence.

Win a Friendly Competition

In addition, Super Liquid Soccer suggests you invite your companions and compete, facing your teams and struggling to achieve the main reward. Make your squad noticeable by creating a vibrant uniform and try to outsmart your buddy, attempting treacherous movements and enjoying the exceptionally amusing physics of the game.
